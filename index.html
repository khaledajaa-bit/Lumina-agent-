Core Intelligence

Three systems, one mind.

Lumina Agent isn't a single model bolted onto a wallet. It's three cooperating systems — perception, judgment, and execution — that hand decisions off to one another in milliseconds, so every position is opened with context and closed with discipline.

Market Perception

Lumina ingests order-book depth, on-chain flow, and cross-venue price action in real time, building a live model of where liquidity is thin, where it's crowded, and where the next move is likely to originate. It doesn't react to candles — it reads the market underneath them.

Risk-Aware Judgment

Every signal is weighed against a strict risk budget before it's allowed near capital. Position sizing, correlation exposure, and drawdown limits are enforced by rule, not vibes — so the agent is built to survive bad weeks, not just win good ones.

Autonomous Execution

Once a decision clears its own risk checks, Lumina routes and executes on-chain directly — no dashboard to babysit, no order to place by hand. Trades settle in the open, verifiable by anyone, block by block.

How It Works

From signal to settlement.

The full loop runs continuously, but it breaks down into four honest steps — nothing hidden behind a black box you're asked to trust blindly.

01

Observe

Streams live order-book, volume, and on-chain data across every venue Lumina is connected to, refreshed multiple times per second.

02

Model

Scores opportunities against a learned model of market regime, then discards anything that doesn't clear a minimum confidence threshold.

03

Check

Runs every surviving idea through fixed risk rules — position limits, exposure caps, drawdown guards — before capital ever moves.

04

Execute

Settles the trade on-chain, logs the outcome, and feeds the result back into the model for the next cycle.

Questions

Before you dive in.

What does Lumina Agent actually trade?

Lumina operates across on-chain liquidity pools and listed pairs reachable from Robinhood Chain, focusing on venues with enough depth for its risk model to size positions responsibly.

Do I need to do anything after buying $LUMINA?

No. Holding the token is the extent of participation required — the agent operates independently of any individual holder's action.

Is the agent's activity verifiable?

Yes. Every trade Lumina executes settles on-chain and is publicly visible, and the contract is indexed and verifiable through Virtuals.io.

What happens during periods of high volatility?

Lumina's risk layer widens its confidence thresholds and reduces position sizing automatically — it is designed to trade less, not harder, when conditions turn unstable.
